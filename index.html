The Challenges Government Faces —
And How PSN Helps Solve Them

Public sector professionals are navigating a period of rapid change. PSN exists to connect them with the ideas, peers, and solutions they need to move forward.

Challenge
Keeping pace with digital transformation
Agencies must modernise legacy systems and adopt AI, cloud, and data tools — often with limited internal capability. PSN provides peer learning, expert speakers, and industry case studies that translate complex technology into practical action.
Challenge
Doing more with constrained budgets
Rising community expectations meet shrinking operational budgets, forcing smarter procurement and delivery. PSN surfaces proven solutions and connects government teams with partners who understand public sector constraints.
Challenge
Breaking down silos across agencies
Collaboration between departments, tiers of government, and sectors is critical — but rarely easy. PSN's cross-agency events and communities create the neutral ground for those conversations to happen.
Challenge
Building and retaining a skilled workforce
Upskilling teams in data, cyber, and digital service delivery is increasingly difficult. PSN's Academy and events equip teams with future-ready skills and cross-sector perspectives.
Challenge
Navigating cyber risk and compliance
Threat surfaces are expanding and compliance frameworks are tightening. PSN's Cyber events connect government security leaders with the knowledge and partners to stay ahead.
Challenge
Delivering citizen-centred services
The public expects seamless, accessible services — but redesigning delivery at scale is complex. PSN showcases real-world CX transformation and creates space to learn from those who've done it.

Three Formats, One Umbrella —
Sponsor What Fits Your Goals

Each Government Innovation Week runs across multiple days with distinct event formats. You can partner across the full week or select the format that best matches your audience and objectives.

Innovation Showcase
Government Innovation Showcase
The centrepiece of each GIW — a full-day conference bringing together senior government leaders and industry for keynotes, case studies, panel discussions, and interactive roundtables focused on the region's most pressing transformation priorities.
FormatFull-day conference
AudienceSenior gov leaders across all levels and functions
Typical size200–400+ delegates
Sponsor opportunitySpeaking slots, exhibition presence, branded roundtables, and post-event delegate insights.
Digital Leadership Day
Digital Leadership Day
A focused, single-day program for government technology and digital leaders exploring how AI, automation, and analytics are reshaping decision-making, service delivery, and workforce capability across the public sector.
FormatSingle-day deep-dive
AudienceCIOs, CDOs, digital directors, IT leads
Typical size100–200 delegates
Sponsor opportunityThought leadership presentations, panel participation, and targeted engagement with technology decision-makers.
Local Gov Focus Day
Local Government Focus Day
A dedicated event for council leaders and local government professionals tackling the unique challenges of service delivery, financial sustainability, and community engagement at the local level — including innovation, AI adoption, and workforce capability.
FormatSingle-day focused forum
AudienceCEOs, GMs, CIOs, and transformation leads from councils
Typical size80–150 delegates
