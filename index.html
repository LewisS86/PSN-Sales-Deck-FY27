Why Partner With PSN

Where Industry
Meets Government

PSN is the trusted, neutral platform where public sector professionals come to learn, connect, and work through the hardest problems they face. As a partner, you don't just get visibility — you get a seat at that table. Every engagement we build starts with your goals, so whether you're looking for a proven format or something entirely bespoke, we'll find the right fit together.

Credibility & Thought Leadership
Be trusted, not just seen

PSN is independent and government-first. When you share a stage with senior public servants, you inherit that trust — not as a vendor, but as a valued part of the conversation. Speaking slots, panels, and content programs let you contribute genuine thinking. Government remembers the partners who helped them understand something, not the ones who just showed up.

Real Relationships & Peer Influence
The most powerful endorsement comes from a peer in the room

Government buys from people it knows. PSN creates the repeated, meaningful touchpoints — roundtables, conversations, shared meals — that turn a name on a badge into a trusted relationship. And when a peer says “it worked for us,” that carries more weight than any campaign.

National Reach, Local Relevance
Federal, state, territory and local — all in one ecosystem

From Canberra to councils, PSN connects you across every tier of government — with events in every major city and a digital platform that works year-round.

Programs Built on Real Intelligence
We know what government is thinking — because we ask them

Every agenda, topic, and speaker is chosen because it reflects what government actually needs to hear — shaped by research partnerships and continuous polling of our 100K+ member network. As a partner, that intelligence works for you too.

Government Innovation Week

Three Formats, One Umbrella —
Sponsor What Fits Your Goals

Each Government Innovation Week runs across multiple days with distinct event formats. You can partner across the full week or select the format that best matches your audience and objectives. These are starting points — not limits. We regularly co-design custom programs with partners who have specific goals or audiences in mind.

Innovation Showcase
Government Innovation Showcase
The flagship event of each GIW — a full-day, tech-focused conference centred on real-world use cases, solution demonstrations, and case studies. Plenary sessions give way to breakout tracks and a buzzing exhibition floor where government meets industry.
FormatPlenary · Breakout tracks · Exhibition floor
AudienceGovernment professionals across all levels
Typical size200–400+ delegates

Digital Leadership Day
Digital Leadership Day
An exclusive, invite-only forum for Director-level and above. No exhibition floor — just strategic conversations between senior government leaders and a select group of trusted solution partners in an intimate setting.
FormatInvite-only · Plenary · Panels · Roundtables
AudienceDirector level and above — government only
Typical size80–100 delegates

Local Government Focus Day
A dedicated, intimate forum addressing the specific challenges facing local government — from service delivery and infrastructure to community engagement and financial sustainability. Honest conversations in a room that understands the unique pressures of council.
